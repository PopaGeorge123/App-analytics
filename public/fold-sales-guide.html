03 — Ideal Customer Profile

Who Fold is built for.

Fold is purpose-built for founders running small businesses with live revenue and multiple tools. The ICP is not an enterprise data team — it's a solo founder or small team making real decisions every week.

SaaS Founders

Running a subscription product. Tracking MRR, churn, CAC, and conversion from Stripe + GA4 + Meta Ads. Need to know immediately when any metric breaks.

DTC / E-commerce

Shopify or WooCommerce store. Running paid ads on Meta or Google. Need to see ad spend vs. revenue in one place and catch ROAS drops before they compound.

Creators & Newsletter

Beehiiv or Mailchimp newsletter + Gumroad / Lemon Squeezy products. Need subscriber growth, open rate trends, and product revenue in one place.

Indie SaaS / Bootstrapped

Paddle or Lemon Squeezy for billing. Plausible or PostHog for analytics. Privacy-conscious. Wants insights without a data stack or analyst.

Agency Owners

Managing clients across Shopify, Mailchimp, Meta Ads. Needs a consolidated view per business without exporting CSVs or building custom reports.

Enterprise teams with dedicated analysts. Businesses with no digital revenue. Anyone looking for a BI tool to build custom reports — Fold does it for you automatically.

06 — Security & Privacy

Your data is safer here than in a spreadsheet.

Security and privacy are first-class concerns at Fold — especially for founders who are hesitant to connect financial tools. Every point below is factual and verifiable.

OAuth 2.0 Only

All connections use OAuth 2.0 — the same standard used by Google and Apple sign-in. No passwords are ever stored, asked for, or transmitted.

Read-Only Access

Fold requests read-only permissions on every integration. It is technically impossible for Fold to create, modify, or delete anything in your connected accounts.

TLS 1.3 Encryption

All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.3 — the current industry standard. No data travels unencrypted between Fold's servers and your browser.

AES-256 at Rest

All stored data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 via Supabase — the same encryption standard used by banks and government agencies.

GDPR Ready

Users can delete their account and all associated data at any time from within the dashboard. No data is retained after deletion.

No Data Selling

Fold never sells, shares, or uses your business data to train models or advertise. Your data belongs to you.

The easiest security objection to handle: "We use the same connection method as when you log in with Google. We request read-only access — Fold can't create a charge, send an email, or change a setting in any of your tools. It literally isn't possible."

Can Fold make changes to my Stripe account?✓ No. Read-only OAuth scope. Write access was never requested.

08 — Objections & How to Answer

Every pushback, handled.

These are the most common objections heard in sales conversations. Use these responses word-for-word or adapt them.

💬 "I already use Google Analytics / Stripe. I have dashboards there."
Those are great tools — but they only show their own data. When revenue drops, you can't tell from Stripe alone whether it was a traffic problem (GA4), an ads problem (Meta), or a product problem (Shopify). Fold connects all of them and crosses the data, so you see the full picture instead of having to assemble it yourself every week.
💬 "I'm worried about connecting my Stripe / financial data."
That's a fair concern — let me explain exactly what happens. Fold uses OAuth, the same technology as "Login with Google." We request read-only access, which means Fold literally cannot create a charge, issue a refund, or change any setting. You can revoke the connection from Stripe's dashboard in 10 seconds at any time. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell or share it.
💬 "I don't have time to set this up."
The average connection time is 90 seconds per integration. You click Connect, log in to the platform, approve read-only access, and you're done. No configuration, no data mapping, no engineers. If you connect Stripe and GA4 right now, you'll have your first insight in under 5 minutes.
💬 "I can just build this in a spreadsheet."
You could — and if you enjoy spending 3–5 hours a week building and maintaining it, that's a valid choice. But every time Stripe, GA4, or Meta changes their export format, the spreadsheet breaks. Fold maintains all the integrations for you. And a spreadsheet can't tell you why something changed — it just shows numbers. Fold gives you the explanation.
💬 "Is this just another dashboard tool? I've tried those."
Most dashboard tools give you charts and leave you to figure out what they mean. Fold goes further — it detects anomalies automatically, explains them in plain English, and tells you what to do. The AI Advisor has the full context of your business across all connected tools. It's the difference between data and insight.
💬 "I'm not sure $19/month is worth it."
That's exactly why there's a 7-day free trial with no credit card required. Connect your tools, use it for a week, and decide. Most founders have their ROI moment in the first 48 hours when they see something they didn't know about — a campaign underperforming, a conversion rate drop, or a churn spike that started 3 days ago.
💬 "The integration I use isn't on your list."
We're adding integrations every week. Tell us which one you need and we'll prioritize it — the founder personally reads every support email. In the meantime, many integrations support API key connections right now, and any integration you need can be on the roadmap within days.
💬 "I'm a small business, I don't need AI."
You don't need to use the AI features — the unified dashboard alone saves most founders their first 3 hours of manual work. But when you do want to know why something happened, the AI is there. It's not an extra thing to learn — you just type a question in plain English and it answers it.
